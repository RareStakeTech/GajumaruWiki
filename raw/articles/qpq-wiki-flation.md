## Inflation, Deflation, Coins, Tokens, and Prices

QPQ emphasizes precise language, noting that media often employs imprecise definitions.

**Key Definitions:**
- **Inflation**: "An increase in the money supply"
- **Deflation**: "A decrease in the money supply"
- **Coin**: In blockchain context, the underlying currency established by a blockchain protocol
- **Token**: In blockchain context, any referential value device
- **Price**: "The amount of a resource, usually money and usually denominated in coin, which willing parties are willing to exchange for a good or service"

**Economic Effects:**
Inflation of the money supply diminishes currency value progressively. The timeline depends on how rapidly monetary distributions flow through an economy and how quickly perceptions about currency value spread. Deflation produces opposite effects.

**Price Dynamics:**
While prices respond to money supply distribution, they are fundamentally determined by perceived value. The mere possibility of future money creation or distribution can substantially influence prices.
