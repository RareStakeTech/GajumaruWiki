## Monetary Mechanics

There are three fundamental approaches to implementing money:

1. **Commodity reference**: Systems using reserve notes and bonds as backing
2. **Debt issuance**: Modern fiat currencies as currently used globally
3. **Ledger-based currency**: Systems like historical Yap Rai stone money or contemporary cryptocurrencies

"There are varying mechanics available that can be used to convey common faith in a future value" — these represent different mechanisms for establishing shared confidence in monetary systems rather than inherently superior or inferior approaches.
