## The Mint Function, Blockchain Inflation, and Currency Debasement

Bitcoin's original whitepaper described "a ledger-based currency that used cryptography to make it impossible to artificially inflate the monetary supply." The system features "a distributed public ledger that would provide permanent traceability of money ownership transfers back to its origin."

Nakamoto's design incorporates predetermined monetary expansion: "early miners" receive "new coins at a greater rate than later miners to get coins into circulation early on," while "later miners" benefit from "more valuable transaction fees as the minting of new coins on the chain slows."
