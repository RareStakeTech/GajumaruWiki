## Proof of Work

In the "hold a competition" case of Leader Selection, a special kind of puzzle is needed with important properties:

1. **Difficult to solve, but easy to verify.** Like a jigsaw puzzle: "it takes a long time to figure out where all the pieces go, but once complete anyone can check whether it is correctly solved by merely glancing at it."

2. **Random outcomes.** "Given a set of equally powerful contenders, the outcome should be random."
