## Bitcoin: The Proof of Concept That Couldn't Scale

Satoshi Nakamoto created Bitcoin to provide a currency that:
- Cannot be printed on command
- Can be traded electronically without intermediation

But Bitcoin failed as everyday currency: 10+ minute checkout waits, fees exceeding the purchase value. **"Bitcoin's limitations meant that it stopped being considered a currency in 2016 and was instead called a store of value."** It opened the door — QPQ walked through it.
