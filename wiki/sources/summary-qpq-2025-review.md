# Summary: Gajumaru 2025 Year in Review & 2026 Prospectives

Published by QPQ AG. Closing remarks by CEO Gregory Chew.

---

## 2025 Milestones

### Applications Launched
- **GajuExplorer** — web-based chain explorer (groot.mainnet.gajumaru.io)
- **Gajumining.com** — account dashboard for mining license purchases
- **GajuDesk** — cross-platform desktop wallet (Windows/macOS/Linux); zero dependencies, original codebase; wallet management, chain interaction, contract dev/deploy/call
- **GajuMine** — cross-platform mining program (Windows/macOS/Linux)

### Currency Launch: October 16, 2025
First actual Gaju token drawdowns from smart contracts. Miners accessed real funds from Hive miners — marks actual currency entering circulation.

### LTIN Announcement: October 22, 2025
Six days after first Gaju drawdowns, [[ltin]] announced:
- Sovereign blockchain infrastructure for Liechtenstein national digital economy
- QPQ as founding technical partner and shareholder; majority ownership: Telecom Liechtenstein
- Operating under Liechtenstein Blockchain Act, EU MiCAR compliant
- Founding participants: QPQ AG, Bank Frick, Bitcoin Suisse, Swiss Subnet, Zilliqa
- Launch timeline: Q2/Q3/Q4 2026

Strategic significance: validates Gajumaru architecture for national-level implementation.

### Revenue & Commercial
- **CHF 1M+** pre-release SaaS mining revenue, zero marketing budget
- Organic, word-of-mouth demand only
- Payment friction resolved: now accepts debit/credit cards and cryptocurrency
- Cecille de Jesus joined marketing team November 2025

## 2026 Deployment Plan

### MainNet Launch: April 6/7, 2026
- Deployed at 23:59:00 Zulu, April 6, 2026
- Delivers full Groot documentation enabling external development (Ethereum-style)
- Permissionless building without QPQ intervention

### Associate Chains
- Independent creation, design, governance per chain
- Native interoperability; no bridges; no third-party trust
- Operational [[ripa-model]]: Resource → Infrastructure → Platform → Application

### 2026 Product Suite

**GajuMobile** (iOS/Android/Linux)
- Direct smartphone smart contract access; zero dependencies
- Use cases: retail payments, banking 1.4 billion unbanked globally

**GajuDEX** — Decentralised Exchange
- Immutable smart contracts; no admin keys; no upgradability
- No governance votes for fee redirects or asset freezing
- First DEX to pass FINMA substance-over-form test for genuine decentralisation
- AMM + CLOB with stop losses; infinitesimal fees; TradingView-like analytics
- Sophia language smart contracts (FINMA compliant)

**GajuMarket** — On-Chain Marketplace
- Soft launch mid-January 2026; world's first native blockchain marketplace
- Supports physical goods and digital assets; peer-to-peer e-commerce
- Asset scope: tangible items to real estate

**GajuPay** — Point-of-Sale Payment System
- Solves shop-level on-chain payment acceptance
- Multi-register routing, real-time fiat/Gaju rate logging (tax compliance), refund chains
- Backend analogous to Stripe's origins

**GajuSafe** — Social Recovery & Key Escrow
- Shamir's Secret Sharing for key recovery via pre-selected trusted contacts
- Prevents permanent asset loss from forgotten keys

**Da Vinci Protocol** — Creative & IP Monetisation
- Addressable market: USD 10.8 trillion
- Enabled: cinema, music, art, patents, athlete NIL, royalties on-chain
- Active outreach to Ligue 1 and La Liga footballers
- AI integration: meta-tagging for LLM licensing (ChatGPT, Grok, Claude)
- Variant: InnovateLoyalty for corporate/retail loyalty

**QPQ Capital AG** — SRO Application (Jan 2026)
- SRO membership under AMLA (precedent: Bitcoin Suisse, Bitpanda)
- Enables: GajuFX (CHF/EUR/USD on-off ramps), GajuVault (custodial recovery), tokenization (Swiss DLT Act 2021), GajuCEX/GajuTrade, KYC Wallets, Mining as Service, Key Management
- MiCAR firm in Liechtenstein or Germany planned for dual Swiss-EU coverage

**Additional 2026 Products:** GajuMe (PayMe), GajuLoyalty, Giggerlicious (gig work/payments), GajuMall (large-scale retailer marketplace)
