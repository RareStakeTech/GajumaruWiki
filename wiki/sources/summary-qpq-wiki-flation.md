# Flation / Inflation, Deflation, Coins, Tokens, and Prices (QPQ Public Wiki)

QPQ's precise monetary vocabulary, authored by [[peter]] (Peter Harpending, last edited 2026-03-17).

## Key Definitions

| Term | QPQ Definition |
|------|---------------|
| **Inflation** | An increase in the money supply |
| **Deflation** | A decrease in the money supply |
| **Coin** | The underlying currency established by a blockchain protocol |
| **Token** | Any referential value device (not the native coin) |
| **Price** | The amount of a resource willing parties will exchange for a good or service |

## Significance for Gaju

Gaju is a **coin** (not a token) — it is the native currency of the Groot protocol. The fixed 1-trillion supply over 87.5 years means the supply increase rate is predetermined and decelerating — a known, bounded inflation schedule, not arbitrary.

The coin/token distinction matters: tokens can be issued by anyone on Associate Chains; Gaju is the mint's native output.
