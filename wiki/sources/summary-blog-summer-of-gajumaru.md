# Summary: The Summer of Gajumaru — Upcoming Launches

Post-MainNet launch announcement article confirming product launch roadmap and confirming MainNet public launch date of April 26, 2026.

---

## MainNet Full Launch Date

**April 26, 2026** — confirmed as the "Mainnet full launch" date. (Compare: April 6/7, 2026 is cited in other sources as the technical launch at 23:59:00 Zulu. Possible distinction: April 6/7 = technical/soft launch; April 26 = public commercial launch event.)

**Satoshi anniversary significance:** April 26, 2011 is the date of Satoshi Nakamoto's last known public message. QPQ deliberately chose April 26, 2026 — the **15th anniversary** — for the public MainNet launch. This is an explicit symbolic statement: Gajumaru as the next phase of the vision Satoshi demonstrated was possible.

## Products Launched / Launching

| Product | Status | Detail |
|---------|--------|--------|
| GajuMarket | Launching | "World's first on-chain marketplace for goods and services"; P2P marketplace sector projected $6.27B by 2032 |
| GajuPay | Launching | Payment processor; enables merchant Gaju acceptance via GajuMobile; payment service provider sector $56.97B (2025) → $64.95B (2026) |
| GajuMobile | Launching | iOS & Android; broader user adoption |
| Associate Chains | Available | Customisable infrastructure for institutions, custodians, government |
| GajuDEX | Launching | FINMA-compliant DEX; AMM + CLOB; targets $877B institutional market |

## Community Incentives

- Testers receive **CHF 100 mining licenses** as rewards for participation
- Mining licenses enable early Gaju earning before public mining (2027)
- **Referral commissions** on license sales
- **Partnership program** for community-driven business onboarding

## Significance

This is the most recent QPQ communications (as of wiki compilation date May 15, 2026). It confirms the Summer 2026 product push, the dual-launch date ambiguity, and the institutional focus of GajuDEX ($877B market).
