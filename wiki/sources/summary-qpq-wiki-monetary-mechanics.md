# Monetary Mechanics (QPQ Public Wiki)

QPQ's foundational monetary theory, authored by [[peter]] (Peter Harpending).

Three fundamental approaches to implementing money:

| Approach | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Commodity reference | Reserve notes and bonds as backing | Gold standard, commodity-backed currencies |
| Debt issuance | Currency as debt obligation | Modern fiat — USD, EUR, CHF |
| Ledger-based | Shared ledger as the source of truth | Yap Rai stone money, Bitcoin, Gaju |

All three represent different mechanisms for establishing "common faith in a future value" — not inherently superior or inferior, but suited to different contexts.
