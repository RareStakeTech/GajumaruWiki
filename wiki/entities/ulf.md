# Ulf (Blockchain Infrastructure Engineer)

Ulf is a blockchain infrastructure engineer at QPQ AG, responsible for the hive mining system and ensuring the Gajumaru network can scale to handle mass participation.

## Background

Ulf has been working in **Erlang** since **1992**, making him one of the longest-standing practitioners in the Erlang community. Gregory Chew (Demo 1, March 2024): "One of the leading lights in the Erlang world... was vital to making all of these pieces work." Gajumaru's implementation in Erlang/OTP is a direct consequence of Ulf's expertise.

## Role

Three distinct contributions identified:

1. **Erlang/OTP implementation** — Gajumaru runs on Erlang/OTP (inherited from Aeternity base). Ulf's 30+ years of Erlang expertise was the enabling factor.

2. **Hive mining concept and implementation** — Designed and built the entire SaaS hive mining infrastructure, including the dividend-writing smart contracts that automatically distribute Gaju rewards to license holders. Gregory Chew described this as a global first: "There was no Reddit forum you could go to to say 'Hey, I have this problem, how do I solve it?'"

3. **Blockchain scalability for mass adoption** — "Ulf has been doing the work to make sure that the blockchain itself, the hive miners and so on can actually handle this" — ensuring the infrastructure can scale from 300 miners to the millions targeted.
