# Ulf (Blockchain Infrastructure Engineer)

Ulf is a blockchain infrastructure engineer at QPQ AG, responsible for the hive mining system and ensuring the Gajumaru network can scale to handle mass participation.

## Role

Two distinct contributions identified:

1. **Hive mining concept and implementation** — Designed and built the entire SaaS hive mining infrastructure, including the dividend-writing smart contracts that automatically distribute Gaju rewards to license holders. Gregory Chew described this as a global first: "There was no Reddit forum you could go to to say 'Hey, I have this problem, how do I solve it?'"

2. **Blockchain scalability for mass adoption** — "Ulf has been doing the work to make sure that the blockchain itself, the hive miners and so on can actually handle this" — ensuring the infrastructure can scale from 300 miners to the millions targeted.
