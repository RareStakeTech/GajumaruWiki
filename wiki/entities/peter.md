# Peter (Communicator) / Peter Harpending (Wiki Author)

There are two people named Peter associated with QPQ AG:

## Peter Harpending — Research Lead & Lead Cryptographic Developer

**Peter Harpending** holds the title **Research Lead and Lead Cryptographic Developer** at QPQ AG. He is a mathematician and cryptographic library developer who has created independent wallet implementations and mobile wallet libraries. He is named explicitly in the Gaju Mining Journey Update as part of the background team: "Peter, Santosh, **Peter Harpending**, Jarvis."

Peter is also the author and editor of the QPQ Public Wiki (`git.qpq.swiss/QPQ-AG/public-wiki/wiki/`), QPQ's primary public educational resource. His wiki covers topics from monetary theory through to the Sophia smart contract language. Last edited: **2026-03-17** (per Blockchain Primer page metadata). Contact: peterharpending@qpq.swiss.

Source: [[summary-qpq-wiki-blockchain-primer]], [[summary-gaju-mining-journey-update]]

## Peter (Video Communicator / Rubber Duck)

A separate Peter appears in the explainer video series as a non-technical communicator, serving as a "rubber duck" for the team. His role is to ask questions a smart non-specialist would ask, stress-test explanations, and represent the target viewer.

Craig describes him as "really valuable" for asking questions like "I'm sorry, this may seem obvious but what about this?" — and the answer is usually that the complexity is the industry's fault (disinformation), not the questioner's.

Peter acknowledges his own journey:
> "One of the complications I had when I got into this industry was there's so many layers of complexity on the technical side and economical side."

**Full name not given in video sources — first name only.** Whether this is the same person as Peter Harpending is unconfirmed.

Source: [[summary-the-gajumaru-an-actual-working-blockchain]]
