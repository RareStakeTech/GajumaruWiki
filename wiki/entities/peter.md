# Peter (Communicator)

Peter is a non-technical communicator at QPQ AG, serving as a "rubber duck" for the team's explainer video series. His role is to ask the questions a smart non-specialist would ask, stress-test explanations before they reach a general audience, and represent the target viewer.

## Role

Craig describes Peter as "really valuable" for asking questions like "I'm sorry, this may seem obvious but what about this?" — and the answer is usually that the complexity is the industry's fault (disinformation), not the questioner's.

Peter explicitly acknowledges his own journey:
> "One of the complications I had when I got into this industry was there's so many layers of complexity on the technical side and economical side."

His presence in the videos models the learning process for the viewer.
