# Benno Raeber (Chief Legal Officer)

**Benno Raeber** is the Chief Legal Officer (CLO) of [[qpq-ag]]. He oversees the legal framework for QPQ AG's operations, including regulatory compliance, patent strategy (GPL3/Red Hat model), and the FINMA/MiCA positioning of GajuDEX.
