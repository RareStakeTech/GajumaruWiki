# QPQ AG

QPQ AG is the Swiss company that builds and operates the Gajumaru blockchain ecosystem. It is the founding technical partner and shareholder of [[ltin]].

## Key People
- **[[gregory-chew]]** — CEO
- **Cecille de Jesus** — Marketing (joined November 2025)

## Jurisdiction & Regulation
- Incorporated in Switzerland
- QPQ Capital AG: SRO application submitted January 2026 (AMLA Self-Regulatory Organisation)
- Precedent: Bitcoin Suisse, Bitpanda
- MiCAR firm planned in Liechtenstein or Germany for EU VASP coverage
- FinTech banking license as longer-term pathway

## Revenue & Commercial
- CHF 1M+ SaaS mining revenue pre-MainNet, zero marketing budget
- Organic demand only; word-of-mouth
- First payment friction resolved December 2025 (now: cards + crypto accepted)

## Products
Builds and operates the full [[gajumaru-product-suite]]: GajuDesk, GajuMine, GajuExplorer, GajuDEX, GajuMarket, GajuPay, GajuSafe, GajuMobile, Da Vinci Protocol, and regulated services under QPQ Capital AG.

## Raises Planned (2026)
- Q2 2026: QPQ AG or QPQ IaaS AG unicorn raise (CHF 50M+ target)
- Q2/Q3 2026: QPQ Capital AG raise

## LTIN Role
QPQ is founding technical partner of [[ltin]] (Liechtenstein Trust Integrity Network). Telecom Liechtenstein holds majority ownership of LTIN; QPQ is shareholder.
