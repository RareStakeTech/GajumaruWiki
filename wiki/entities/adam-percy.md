# Adam Percy (Community Champion)

Adam Percy is QPQ AG's primary community champion — the first and most active member of the Gajumaru mining community, described by Gregory Chew as "an incredible gift."

## Background

Adam Percy is a **house builder** by profession — not a software engineer or crypto-native. His adoption of Gajumaru from day one, and his ability to master it, validate the accessibility claim: if a house builder can understand and evangelize Gajumaru, it is genuinely accessible to non-technical audiences.

## Role in the Community

From [[summary-gaju-mining-journey-update]]:

> "He's a house builder. You're going to have to simplify this. [He became] the best advocate we could possibly imagine to have within our community, who's proactively rolling up his sleeves getting stuck in to help everybody who comes in the door."

Gregory Chew (likely speaker):
> "I'm extraordinarily proud of... Adam Percy, what an incredible gift."

Adam Percy:
- Arrived "day one" — first wave of the pre-release protected mining community
- Became a proactive community helper, assisting new miners with onboarding
- QPQ's named community champion ("Adam Percy — Community champion" in team roster)

## Significance

Adam Percy represents QPQ's thesis that [[protected-mining]] and Gajumaru's UX are accessible to everyday people, not just crypto specialists. His transition from confused newcomer to active community leader validated the product's usability before MainNet.

> "Our early users... were the guinea pigs for all of the developments... Their feedback was extraordinarily valuable because it meant that we learned things now."
